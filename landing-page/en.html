WHY DDM CONNECT

We Connect Complex Market Information Easily

Solve the inconveniences you experienced at Dongdaemun Market with our online platform.

Difficulty Finding Products

Dongdaemun Market has a wide variety of products, but it is very difficult for first-time visitors to find the products they want among countless stores.

Lack of Online Promotion

Even stores with great products and competitiveness have difficulty meeting new buyers or individual customers if they lack online exposure.

Inconvenient Inquiry Process

It is difficult to check product information, exact location, and contact information before visiting the market, which often wastes time and money.

OUR SOLUTIONS

Core Features Connecting Stores and Customers

Check out the convenient features provided by DDM Market Connect.

Store Product Registration

Stores can easily register and manage product images, detailed descriptions, price ranges, categories, and contact information themselves.

Explore by Category

Easily find the products you need by detailed categories such as fabrics, buttons, zippers, lace, accessories, and craft materials.

Check Info Before Visiting

Visitors can check the desired products and the exact location (building/floor/room) of the store in advance for an efficient market tour.

Easy Inquiry Connection

We provide a direct structure that allows you to directly inquire with the store of the product you are interested in via phone, KakaoTalk, or email.
